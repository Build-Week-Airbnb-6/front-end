What is AirBnB Optimal Price?

Using historical booking data from AirBnB, AirBnB Optimal Price will allow you to predict the trends for optimal pricing for their properties based on variables such as location, time of year and other considerations. You will be able to manage multiple properties at different locations and be able to make adjustments to their expectations for booking, as well as what you should be charging.

How do we calculate our price recommendations?

We utilize historical AirBnB data to build machine learning models that will accurately deliver price info with a user-friendly, simple interface.

Who uses AirBnB Optimal Price?

Anyone who owns or manages AirBnB rental properties.
